The Market Opportunity

Government is Brazil's biggest buyer — and it's completely opaque

Brazilian governments spend over R$1.4 trillion (~$280B USD) per year in public procurement. Yet 200,000+ B2G companies still discover opportunities manually — copy-pasting from 3 disconnected portals, missing 70% of relevant tenders.


Lei 14.133 (2021) modernized the legal framework but created new complexity: PNCP, ComprasGov, and PCP now operate with different schemas, schedules, and APIs. SmartLic is the unification layer.
