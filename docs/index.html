The Market Opportunity

Government is Brazil's biggest buyer — and it's completely opaque

Brazilian federal, state, and municipal governments spend over R$1.4 trillion (~$280B USD) per year in public procurement. Yet 200,000+ B2G companies still discover opportunities manually — copy-pasting from 3 disconnected portals, missing 70% of relevant tenders.


Lei 14.133 (2021 Public Procurement Act) modernized the legal framework but created new complexity: PNCP, ComprasGov, and PCP now operate with different schemas, schedules, and APIs. SmartLic is the unification layer.

Competitive Moat

Four compounding defensible advantages

Every active user makes the product more defensible — through data accumulation, SEO compounding, and network effects.

Proprietary DataLake

1.5M+ active bids + 2M+ historical contracts crawled and normalized. 400-day retention enables longitudinal market intelligence that competitors can't replicate quickly. Full-text search at <100ms p95 without live API calls.

AI Classification Engine

Proprietary keyword density scoring + GPT-4.1-nano zero-match classification per sector. 20 sectors with exclusion lists, context-required keywords, and viability value ranges. Trained on real government tender language — not generic NLP.

SEO Inbound Flywheel

10,000+ programmatic ISR pages (observatório, CNPJ intelligence, sector reports, municipal index) convert organic search intent into trial signups. Growing compounding asset — each new contract in the DataLake creates new indexed pages.

Regulatory Complexity Barrier

Lei 14.133 (2021) created a fragmented compliance landscape. SmartLic abstracts 3 government APIs with different schemas, authentication models, and rate limits. Deep knowledge of PNCP field semantics (e.g., tamanhoPagina=50 hard cap) is non-trivial to replicate.
